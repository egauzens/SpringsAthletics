Classes

CROSSFIT

This is a 1-hour class comprised of a group warm-up followed by skill or strength training programmed in advance with total body fitness in mind. Emphasis is on building strength, increasing mobility and flexibility and learning proper safety techniques while working out. It is occassionally followed by a complimentary accessory workout to isolate individual muscles

BOOTCAMP

Bootcamp routines are made with the purpose of burning a high amount of calories in a short period of time and getting results quickly, and this workout is no different. Specifically targeting the butt and legs, this 1-hour Booty Bootcamp class is designed to burn fat, tone, and shape to give you legs that will stop traffic.
